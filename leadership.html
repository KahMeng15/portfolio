Leadership

Making sure everything is in place and smoothly executed

Creating Impact and Change for Now and the Future

Small actions can also have a big impact

I believe that whatever I do, it's not just about my growth, but also the growth of others. I strive to turn inconveniences into opportunities, set clear guidelines, and ensure that processes are as efficient as possible. Change doesn’t have to be grand or visible—sometimes, the smallest overlooked details create the biggest silent impacts.

One example of this is my role in the College Representative Council. I noticed a lack of clear guidelines for both multimedia and paperwork, which often led to confusion and inefficiencies. My first initiative was to create templates and guideline documents to standardize the design and documentation process. This not only streamlined workflows but also made it easier for students to organize events efficiently.
