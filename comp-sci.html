Pursuing a Career in Computer Science

I am pursuing a Diploma in Computer Science at Universiti Putra Malaysia Bintulu Campus.

I started my studies here in August 2023 in Borneo and expect to graduate by the end of 2025. My time here has given me valuable knowledge and a stepping stone into the world of computer science, starting with Java as my first programming language. From there, I continued learning more languages and concepts, deepening my understanding of the field.

I have consistently achieved straight A’s and made the Dean’s List throughout my studies, with my first semester placing me on the Vice Chancellor's List. I am dedicated to learning how everything works, understanding how systems are built, and mastering the fundamentals of technology.

I foster a collaborative learning environment with my coursemates, sharing knowledge with those who need guidance while also seeking help when I face challenges. This continuous exchange of knowledge helps me develop better teamwork skills and strengthens my ability to work together to solve problems and build projects.
